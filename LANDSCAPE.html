The Result: An Intuitive 5-Star Rating

The system outputs a final quality index between 1 and 5 stars, providing a quick and intuitive overview of ticket quality for teams and managers.

Rating 1
Weak. The ticket is unclear, lacks user focus, or has no acceptance criteria. Requires significant revision.
Rating 2
Fair. The ticket has major issues in at least one key area. Clarification and rework needed.
Rating 3
Acceptable. The ticket is understandable but could be improved in clarity, user focus, or testability.
Rating 4
Good. A solid, well-formed ticket with only minor room for improvement. Mostly ready for development.
Rating 5
Excellent. A well-structured, user-focused ticket with clear and testable acceptance criteria. Ready for development.
