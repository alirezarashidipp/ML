The Solution

Automated Quality Gatekeeper

An AI-driven system that acts as an always-on analyst. It reads every single JIRA ticket the moment it is created and objectively scores it against Global Agile Standards.

Objectivity

Replacing subjective human opinion ("I think this is clear") with objective mathematical scoring.

Standardization

Ensuring that whether a ticket is written in London, Hong Kong, or Poland, it meets the same high standard.

Efficiency

Stopping ambiguous requirements before they reach developers, preventing the "ping-pong" that slows down sprints.

Beyond Scoring

Smart Remediation & Coaching

We don't just find problems; we fix them. The system acts as a real-time agile coach for your team.

Actionable Feedback Loops

We provide constructive guidance for every submission. Instead of a simple "Rejected", users get specific reasons:

"This ticket lacks a clear 'WHY'. Please specify the business value for the end-user."

Generative AI Remediation

For low-quality tickets, the system automatically rewrites them into a "Gold Standard" format so the user learns by example.
